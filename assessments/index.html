No more “maturity models”
No more aimless pentests

It's time for assessments that make a difference.

Our industry is littered with maturity assessments gathering dust for years and pentest reports that have just been risk-accepted to avoid impacting delivery time trying to fix ill-defined issues.

You need to know if how you're delivering software is the best way for your organisation to meet customers’ needs without compromising their safety and if the efforts of your teams so far have produced sufficiently secure products.

We created the Product Security Framework to be the secure, and adaptable, delivery model that really "shifts left" security into the capable hands of the people designing, building and operating your software products.

Measuring your alignment with this framework for secure software delivery and combining it with our structured product security assessments (based on the OWASP ASVS and accredited by CREST) gives you the insight you need.

Product Security Framework

A secure way of working for your technical delivery organisation. With the visibilty and insight to ensure your software products are built and operated to the right level of security quality.

Well defined.
Provides clear responsibilities and accountabilities to drive scalable and effective delivery of secure products.
Delivery-focused.
Created by experts in delivery to align with and improve modern software product development, not to be a security wishlist.
Objective.
Measures the real outcome, secure software products delivered, with structured OWASP ASVS assessments.
Continuous improvement.
Embeds data-driven feedback to drive not just more secure products but better ways of delivering secure products.
Industry Standards.
Mapped to NIST SSDF, PCI SSLC, OWASP SAMM and BSIMM, giving you demonstrable security best practice.
Scalable.
Designed to be used by organisations with a single product team or a thousand.
