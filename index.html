How We Work

With engineering teams.

We were founded on solid engineering principles and experience. Our practitioners have decades of experience in software engineering and speak credibly to teams across all industries.

Security knowledge is not to be held by a select few. We work across all of your engineering teams to instil the knowledge and approaches necessary to secure engineering at global scale.

With security teams.

We understand the demands on security teams in modern organisations. Our founding partners have worked to protect companies in varied security roles since the early days of the internet.

The approach we bring frees up your overloaded security professionals to focus on higher value work for your organisation by engaging the whole engineering function to build in product security.

With product management teams.

The people making decisions on the products you build and deliver to your customers are critical to the security of your organisation.

Security is just one aspect of overall product quality and we bring a holistic, balanced view on securing products through our work with your product management teams. Our coaching raises the quality of both the products you deliver and how you're delivering them through measurement and positive feedback.

With leadership teams.

Structuring your organisation to instil the right motivations to secure product development is essential. Many well-meaning initiatives to improve security fail due to antagonistic organisational structures.

We work closely with your leadership team to ensure responsibilities and accountabilities are in the right place and that delivery processes are secure. Surfacing our learning and discoveries from delivery teams also brings essential information to organisational decision makers.
