Foundations of Product Security for Decision Makers

Secure Foundations Group Exercises 10-12 participants
  • 4 - 7 hours
  • Remote or on-site

Establish the foundations of delivering secure products for product owners, project managers, business analysts, architects, all the people that connect your products to business value and customer experience. They're critical to the security of your organisation's products and services.

Secure by Design With Agile Threat Modelling

Secure Product Secure Engineering 3-6 players Up to 6 observers
  • 4 hours
  • Remote or on-site

A hands-on, whole team workshop that applies agile threat modelling techniques to the team's product to produce actionable security work on the product backlog. Utilising OWASP Cornucopia or Elevation of Privilege card games to focus on common areas of security weakness within systems and platforms, this intensive workshop enables the team to continue with regular, lightweight, agile threat modelling as part of their own delivery cadence.

Find Out More

Secure Coding Techniques & Practices

Secure Engineering 10-12 Participants Hands-on with real apps Online platform supporting all languages
  • 4-7 hours
  • Remote or on-site

A hands-on, practical secure coding workshop utilising an online platform that provides real development environments with vulnerable applications as labs. Participants will be required to confirm the vulnerabilities by exploiting them themselves, then go on to locate the security issue in the code and fix it. Without breaking any of the app's functionality.
